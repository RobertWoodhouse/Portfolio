About

I’m a software developer from London, England. I started coding at the age of 7 when I stumbled across the instructions manual of my older brothers Atari ST, there was a picture of a pixelated ninja standing on tatami mats with lines of code on the adjacent page and I thought to myself “so this is how you make a game!”.

My passion is developing so I am always working on side projects; I’m currently working on a comic strip reading app using Xamarin to commemorate the return of the legendary strip “The Boondocks”.

C# is my language of choice and I have projects with WPF, Unity, ASP.NET etc... but give me a good book, Stack Overflow, and a scripting API and I’m comfortable with any OOP language.
